SPADATAS BEST PRACTICE: ITALY/DENMARK

Collaboration between Denmark and Italy

Danish students produced a large amount of prototypes on courses in GDPR, privacy and more to both teachers, parents, students, peers and more. These were distributed to many places, amongst these a school in Vercelli, North of Italy. These apps served as an importent source of inspiration to italian upper-primary students and spring 2024 we made an online meeting, where producers and users could meet, discuss, and share experiences.
